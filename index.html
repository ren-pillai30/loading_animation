Advanced Network Infrastructure

This assignment explores the critical importance of network segmentation in modern architecture. By dividing a network into smaller, isolated sections, administrators can contain potential breaches and prevent lateral movement by malicious actors.

We will examine various protocols including TLS 1.3, the implementation of hardware security modules (HSM), and the role of Artificial Intelligence in predictive threat detection. The goal is to build a resilient system that can withstand both automated bot attacks and targeted social engineering attempts.
